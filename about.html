    About Me

    I'm an ASIC Design Engineer at Ericsson, and graduated from Bucknell University with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering.

    I primarily play singleplayer and coop games, got a Quest 2 for beatsaber and other VR games.

    Some Skills

    I primarily program in Python or C, depending on what I am trying to do. I'd say I'm pretty intermediate with both of those languages. I have coded with Bash, C++, Java, Swift, and other more esoteric languages and can use them but they aren't my "bread and butter" languages.

    I'm a bit of an electronics hobbiest, I've worked with simple components (think resistors, caps, leds, switches, etc.) and 74xx ICs. I have a raspberry pi 3b+, arduino uno, ESP32 dev board, and a basys 3 fpga.

    I can read component datasheets, have very basic soldering skills, and would consider myself very proficient at knowing how to use google and new for 2023 chatGPT to search for information.

    If this website is anything to go by I clearly am not much for graphic design, although after 3 or 4 complete re-designs I think this is an ok balance of simple while still being a step above pure-HTML.
